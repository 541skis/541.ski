Our Story

541 was started out of necessity and a love of skiing. Owners, Brad Duffy and Jesse Scroggins, were born into ski racing roots which attributes to their desire to charge the mountain full tilt. In search for a ski to live up to their expectations as an “all mountain” one-quiver, they were consistently left disappointed. So, Brad, designer and production engineer, and Jesse, craftsman and amateur designer, teamed up to create a ski to fill the void. The Adobo was born—541’s flagship ski. Every design is built, tested and proven on the year-round slopes of Mt. Hood, OR by the owners. If we don’t like it, we don’t sell it! 541 skis will not let you down.
